Minesweeper

Instructions

To beat the game, you have to use the numbers that you will gradually uncover on the board to figure out where the bombs are.

There are two ways to win:

  1. Flag all the bombs.
  2. Uncover all the non-bomb cells.

The number of flags left tells you the number of bombs present on the board.
To place or remove a flag, use Ctrl + click.
